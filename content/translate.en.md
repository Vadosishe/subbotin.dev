**Functions: The AI generates topics and scripts, writes the narrator's text, creates videos (AI avatar or based on a human video), selects background and visuals, provides voiceover, and automatically edits the video (9:16, subtitles, music).**

```
The system also generates descriptions, hashtags, and automatically publishes the videos on social media according to a schedule.
```

> Process: topic → script → text → video generation → editing → description → auto-posting.

1. The goal is to create a "content factory" that can produce 5–30 videos per day automatically.
